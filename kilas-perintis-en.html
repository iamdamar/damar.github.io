No 1 Brief

As a graphic designer for Kilas Perintis, my main role involves creating visually appealing social media content and event certificates for various events, collaborating with the copywriting team.

No 2 Challenges

  • Brand Consistency: Ensuring all designs adhere to Kilas Perintis brand guidelines while remaining unique and engaging.
  • Tight Deadlines: Managing multiple projects simultaneously with short turnaround times to meet event schedules.
  • Engagement and Impact: Creating designs that effectively convey messages and motivate the audience to take action, such as donating or participating in events.


No 3 Goals

  • Increasing Awareness: Enhancing the visibility of Kilas Perintis events and initiatives through engaging social media visuals.
  • Boosting Engagement: Designing content that encourages higher levels of interaction on social media platforms.
  • Supporting Fundraising Efforts: Assisting in achieving the organization's fundraising targets through compelling and persuasive designs.

No 4 Solutions

  • Research and Coordination: Conducting research on the latest design trends and audience preferences to ensure relevance and appeal. Collaborating with the copywriting team to understand the message and primary objectives of each event.
  • Design Execution: Using Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop to create a series of cohesive Instagram posts and event certificates. Focusing on clarity, aesthetic appeal, and adherence to brand guidelines.
  • Customization for Events: Tailoring designs to reflect the unique themes and goals of each event while maintaining overall brand consistency.

No 5 Outcome

During eight different events, my designs significantly contributed to Kilas Perintis' achievement in raising funds amounting to Rp6.7 million.

Social media posts experienced increased engagement rates, including likes, comments, and shares, which helped expand the organization's reach and impact.
