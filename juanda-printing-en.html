No 1 Brief

As a graphic designer at Juanda Printing, I create various custom design solutions for our clients, including business cards, brochures, food packaging, invitations, and other branding materials. My role is crucial in enhancing our clients' visual identity through innovative design work.

No 2 Challenges

  • Inefficient Design Process: The existing design workflow is time-consuming and lacks standardization, resulting in delays and increased production costs.
  • Client's Brand Consistency: Clients have diverse branding needs, making it challenging to maintain consistent brand quality and representation across various types of print materials.
  • Resource Limitations: Resource limitations and high production costs make it difficult to deliver high-quality designs without exceeding the budget.


No 3 Goals

  • Providing high-quality custom print materials that align with the brand identity and marketing objectives of each client.
  • Maintaining consistency and quality across all design outcomes.
  • Improving design process efficiency to reduce production costs and increase turnaround time.

No 4 Solutions

  • Custom Design Creation: Developing unique and custom designs for business cards, brochures, food packaging, invitations, and other branding materials. Each design is carefully crafted to reflect the client's brand and appeal to their target audience. I am capable of producing 10 unique designs in one week, ensuring fast and efficient delivery.
  • Quality and Consistency: Adjusting the type of paper used for each product to match the client's quality expectations. This involves regular client feedback loops and adjustments to refine the final product.
  • Process Optimization: Optimizing designs with layout and margin usage that align with the capabilities of printing machines, resulting in a 20% increase in printing efficiency. This significantly reduces production costs and speeds up delivery times.

No 5 Outcome

Through these efforts, Juanda Printing successfully obtained five large repeat orders in the first month of business.

This indicates high satisfaction from clients and their trust in the quality and efficiency of the services we offer.
